Why problem solving

The well-established skill of solving problems can be really useful even in real life. It allows you to calmly approach and break down your difficulties with ease. Instead of choosing the not-always-working brute-force method, you can take a different way. Just split your problem into smaller chunks and solve them one by one instead. Problem-solving is the ability to choose the right method for the right difficulty. By doing this one can later solve it much easier. Also, the way that you solve your problems is important too.
